PyExpo'24

Event Schedule

Phase 1 - IDEA2HACK [ Ideate ]

( Mar 6th ), 2024
IDEA2HACK is an ideathon which isn't just any brainstorming session ‚Äì it's a high-energy, adrenaline-pumping marathon of creativity. A student can be capable of making solution that will be innovative to the given problem statement. At the end of this event student will have a idea to apporach the solution as a product.

Phase 2 - HACKSHOP2EXPO [ Illustrate ]

( Mar 7th - 12th ), 2024
HACKSHOP2EXPO is a workshop , where the mentors from industries will teach the students about technical and non technical skills.This workshop will guide the students how to complete the project from scratch.

Phase 3 - HACK2EXPO [ Iterate ]

( Mar 13th & 14th ), 2024
HACK2EXPO is the main event where students will be working iteratively for 30 hours to complete a project, aiming for an efficient solution to the given problem statement. The student team will receive guidance from a mentor throughout the process.üöÄ

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open for Students from all the departments of First Year . It is the right place for anyone who's interested in learning and innovating with their ideas in Python.

01

Can i form my team with other department students?

NO, Interdisciplinary is not accepted at PyExpo'24. As per the rules, you can form teams of 4-5 members from your department only.

02

How can I apply?

The registration would be simply done by just clicking the Apply with Devfolio button. Click here and Register your team details in DevFolio. Registrations end on 5th March 2024

03

What if I don't know how to code?

No worries, Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level.

04

What are the prizes to be won?

We are going to announce some really exciting prizes and incentives soon. Stay tuned for further updates!
