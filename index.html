About PyExpo'24

PyExpo'24

PyExpo is an annual hackathon organized by IPS-Tech Team , KGiSL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY. It is a 30 hrs hackathon conducted exclusively for the I st year students to enhance their real-world problem-solving skills using Python Programming.

  • Date: 6th March to 14th March 2024
  • Team Size: 4 to 6 members
  • Exclusively for I st - YEAR STUDENTS
  • Mentor Guidance available
  • Participation Certificate available
  • Tracks and Workshops are conducted for learning
  • Amazing Cash Prizes and IoT kits are awaiting for the winners
  • Then, What are you waiting for???

FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions!

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open for Students from all the departments of First Year . It is the right place for anyone who's interested in learning and innovating with their ideas in Python.

01

Can i form my team with other department students?

NO, Interdisciplinary is not accepted at PyExpo'24. As per the rules, you can form teams of 4-6 from your department only.

02

How can I apply?

The registration would be simply done by just clicking the Apply with Devfolio button. Click here and Register your team details in DevFolio. Registrations end on 5th March 2024

03

What if I don't know how to code?

No worries, Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level.

04

What are the prizes to be won?

We are going to announce some really exciting prizes and incentives soon. Stay tuned for further updates!

05

Can we apply as a team?

Yes! We believe that you’re stronger as a team than apart. You can form teams of 4-6 members. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and development skills.
